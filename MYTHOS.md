# Mythos — Tuner

A tuner is not a product. It's a utility. Like a screwdriver, or a ruler, or a metronome — it does one thing and the value is purely in whether it works.

The web is full of tuners that want something in return. Ads. Sign-ups. Upsells to a "pro" version of a thing that should just exist. The tuner becomes a trap. You came to tune a guitar and now you're looking at a banner ad.

Tuner at tuner.obfusco.us does not want anything. Open it, tune, close it.

The ambition here is total lack of ambition — to be the tuner that disappears, that becomes the one you reach for because it doesn't get in the way. Every instrument, every tuning, no account required, no monetization, no plan.

Just: are you in tune? Now you are.
